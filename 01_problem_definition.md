# Business Problem Definition

## Company context
The company operates in the tourism sector and offers online booking services to international customers.
The management team has noticed fluctuations in sales performance and wants to better understand what drives revenue and customer behavior.

## Business problem
Despite stable traffic, revenue has been inconsistent over the last 12 months.
Management lacks clear insights into which customer segments and sales channels generate the highest value.

## Key business questions
1. How has revenue changed over time?
2. Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
3. Which sales channels perform best?
4. Are there seasonal patterns affecting sales?
5. What trends or anomalies can be observed in recent months?

## Key KPIs
- Total revenue
- Revenue growth rate
- Number of bookings
- Average order value (AOV)
- Revenue by customer segment
- Revenue by sales channel

## Expected outcome
The goal of this analysis is to provide data-driven insights that will support strategic decision-making and help improve business performance.
